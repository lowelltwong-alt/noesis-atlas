# Contributor Review Policy

## Review levels

| Risk | Example | Review required |
|---|---|---|
| Low | typo, formatting, citation metadata | maintainer |
| Medium | new branch node, minor source tier update | branch reviewer |
| High | authority structure, canonical/root source, branch governance | branch head + steward |
| Critical | Logos export, license, use permissions, AI religion authority | owner/arbiter + steward + specialist reviewer |

## Conflict of interest

Reviewers should disclose if they are advocating for a branch, opposing a branch, or relying on AI-generated summaries.
