# Faithful Representation Policy

Noesis Atlas should not claim to speak for a living religion, sect, school, or lineage without branch-appropriate review.

## Representation standards

A branch profile must:

- use branch-native categories where possible;
- state its scope and limits;
- identify internal variants;
- cite primary/authoritative sources first;
- distinguish official, scholarly, community, and AI-extracted sources;
- mark uncertainty;
- avoid hostile caricature.

## Reviewer model

For living religious traditions, high-confidence promotion requires a reviewer who is either:

- a recognized practitioner/scholar inside that branch;
- a credentialed scholar of that branch;
- a branch head approved by Noesis governance.

If such a reviewer is unavailable, the branch remains provisional.
